Eligibility

    The candidate should be a citizen of India.
    The candidate should have passed SSLC/THSLC or equivalent examination, with eligibility for higher studies.
    Candidates who have passed qualifying examination with Mathematics, English and Science subjects are eligible to apply.
    For CBSE / ICSE candidates, the Marks / Grades obtained in Science is considered for calculating grade in Science, in the place of Physics and Chemistry. In the case of CBSE candidates, only the certificates of Board Examination shall be accepted as qualifying certificate and such candidates shall submit an Undertaking at the time of admission and a copy of the same shall be submitted along with the Application.


    Scholarship Schemes

      100% Scholarship for meritorious students having 90% marks in PCM & English in 10% of seats in all branches.
      Govt. Merit Fee prescribed for Self-financing institutes, for Students having 65% and above marks in PCM & English.


    The selection will be made on the basis of the candidate's performance (grade) in Mathematics and Science (Physics and Chemistry), in the qualifying examination, in addition to the grade obtained in individual subjects.
    The score declared by the candidate in the application form will be considered for selection. Candidates should attach a copy of the score sheet of the qualifying examination along with the application form.
    Selection of the candidates for interview for admission will be on the basis of information including that of marks furnished by the candidates in the application. So the candidates are advised to be careful while filling the application form.

Our Facilities

Students are provided with facilities for Smithy, Carpentry, Foundry and basic Electrical Works.

World Class Library

A collection of nearly 5000 books on relevant technology subjects is available at the library. The library also subscribes to a variety of national and international technical journals. Since it is open from 9.00 a.m. to 5.00 p.m. on all working days, students can make full use of the facility.

Largest Play Ground

We provide our students and staff with a football court and badminton court as we believe that physical fitness is not only one of the most important keys to a healthy body, it is the basis of dynamic and creative intellectual activity.

Advanced Machinery

Our Electrical Lab is equipped with DC series motor, DC Shunt Motor, Motor Generator set, Slip Ring Induction Motor, Transformers, Universal Motor, Single Phase Induction Motor etc., for students to conduct various experiments..

III. Eligibility for Admission

  • The candidate should be a citizen of India.
  • The candidate should be a native of Kerala or have undergone the qualifying examination from an institution in Kerala State.
  • The candidate should have passed +2/equivalent examination/VHSE with Mathematics, English, physics and chemistry after completion of 2 years regular course or passed 2 years metric courses of NCVT/SCVT/KGCE.
  • In the case of +2/VHSE, the candidate should have a minimum score of 50% in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics separately
  • In the case of 2 years courses of NCVT/SCVT/KGCE candidates should have a minimum of 50% of the aggregate marks in the qualifying examination
  • In the case of candidates who have passed 2 years courses of NCVT/SCVT/KGCE, they will be considered for admission to the related diploma programme only as per the list of ITI/ /KGCE trade and related diploma programmes

IV.General Conditions

  • Candidates who have taken more than 2 chances to pass the qualifying examination are not eligible for admission through lateral entry. However 'SAY' or betterment Examination will not be considered as an additional chance.
  • Candidates who are undergoing any Diploma Programme in a Polytechnic College or having a Diploma are not eligible to apply.
