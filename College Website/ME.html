DIPLOMA IN MECHANICAL ENGINEERING


The Mechanical Engineering department of SCMS College of Polytechnics will be a premier choice for the talented, prospective students learners which is well recognized by most employers for job opportunities because of its comprehensive students-centric learning approach by imparting fundamental knowledge of Engineering and Technology to excel in Mechanical Engineering education and specialized training in an integrated manner through workshops, laboratory sessions, assignments, seminars, internships, projects and independent study.

The primary focus of the department is to impart technical know-how to students, promote their problem solving skills by the blended mixture of excellent academic credential holding faculty members and lab staff with highest skills. The department Establish itself as an excellent academic center through separate Lab block which includes state-of-the-art conventional laboratories such as Workshop, Machine Shop, Hydraulics Lab, Maintenance Lab, Heat Engines Lab, CAD center with advanced licensed software and a department library.


Association of Innovative Mechanical Engineers (AIME)


To Exhibit ethical code of conduct in a professional manner the department is having its own Association of Innovative Mechanical Engineers (AIME) which serves as good platforms for the mechanical engineering community members to meet with each other and to exchange innovative ideas to uplift their knowledge level and update with latest technologies that is ever changing. The association conducts seminars and programmes to give an exposure for various skill development courses related to Mechanical Engineers
